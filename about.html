About Us

Jethani and Malani is a well-established stock trading company based out of Cuffe Parade, Mumbai. The company was founded in 1938 by Mr. Asoklal Malani and Champaklal Jethani, and has since grown into one of the leading stock trading companies in India. With a strong foundation built on decades of experience and expertise, Jethani and Malani have earned a reputation for their exceptional services and deep knowledge of the market. The company offers a wide range of services to its clients, including equity trading, derivatives, commodities, and currency trading. Jethani and Malani cater to the diverse needs of traders and investors, providing valuable insights and analysis to help their clients make informed investment decisions. The company's commitment to sharing knowledge and empowering its clients with the right tools and resources has set it apart in the industry. Over the years, Jethani and Malani have built a loyal clientele who trust the company's expertise and guidance. With a team of experienced professionals, the company is well-equipped to navigate the complex and ever-changing landscape of the stock market. Jethani and Malani continue to strive towards excellence, staying true to their core values of integrity, transparency, and client satisfaction
